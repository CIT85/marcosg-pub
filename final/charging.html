Charging Your Tesla

Charging Infrastructure and Costs

Charging your Tesla is a seamless and efficient experience thanks to Tesla's extensive charging infrastructure. Here's what you need to know about charging your electric vehicle:

Charging Network Overview

Tesla has strategically placed Supercharger stations along major highways and in key urban areas, providing high-speed charging for long-distance travel and convenience.

Additionally, Destination Chargers are available at hotels, restaurants, and shopping centers, allowing you to charge while you enjoy your time at various destinations.

Charging Costs

Charging costs can vary depending on factors such as location and time of use. Tesla provides transparent and competitive pricing for Supercharger usage. You can view the current rates in the Tesla app or on the Tesla website.

For home charging, take advantage of cost-effective overnight charging with a Tesla Wall Connector. Calculate your estimated charging costs based on your electricity rates and driving habits.

Charging Tips

  • Plan Your Route: Utilize the Tesla navigation system to plan your route and locate charging stations along the way for a smooth journey.
  • Schedule Charging: Take advantage of scheduled charging to optimize your charging times and benefit from lower electricity rates.
  • Home Charging Convenience: Install a Tesla Wall Connector at home for the convenience of charging overnight and waking up to a fully charged vehicle.

Experience the freedom of electric driving with Tesla's reliable and widespread charging network.
