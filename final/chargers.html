Charging Your Tesla

Charging methods and Locations

Explore the different types of charging machines and locations available for charging your Tesla:

Supercharger Stations

Tesla Supercharger stations are designed for high-speed charging on long-distance routes. They are strategically located along major highways and in key urban areas, providing a fast and convenient charging experience.

Destination Chargers

Destination Chargers are available at various locations such as hotels, restaurants, and shopping centers. These chargers offer a convenient way to charge your Tesla while you enjoy your time at different destinations.

Home Charging with Tesla Wall Connector

Charge your Tesla at home with the Tesla Wall Connector. It provides a cost-effective and convenient solution for overnight charging. Install the Wall Connector at your home for the flexibility of charging at your convenience.
